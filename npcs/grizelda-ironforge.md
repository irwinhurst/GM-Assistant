# Grizelda Ironforge - Minor NPC

Name: Grizelda Ironforge
Type: Minor NPC
Age: Middle Ages
Race: Mountain Dwarf
Languages: Common, Dwarvish
Class: Fighter (retired)
Occupation: Weapons shop owner/blacksmith

Physical Description: A sturdy mountain dwarf with calloused hands marked by decades of smithing work. Her graying beard is braided with small iron rings, and she wears a leather apron over practical clothing. Burn scars on her forearms tell stories of countless hours at the forge. Her eyes are sharp and appraising when examining weapons or customers.

Personality: Gruff but fair, with little patience for time-wasters or hagglers. Takes immense pride in her craftsmanship and can become animated when discussing weapon quality or technique. Respects those who show genuine appreciation for fine weaponwork.

History: Former city guard who opened "Ironforge Arms" in Waterdeep's Trades Ward after retiring from active duty. Built her reputation through honest dealing and superior weapon maintenance. Knows the weight and balance of every piece in her shop.

Motivation: Maintain her shop's reputation for quality and serve customers who appreciate fine weapons. Wants to pass on traditional dwarven smithing techniques to a worthy apprentice someday.

Party Relationship: Contact
Attitude: Professional

## Interaction Guide

**Greeting**: "Aye, what brings you to my shop? Looking for something that'll last more than a tavern brawl, I hope."

**When examining weapons**: Grizelda insists on demonstrating proper grip and balance, sharing technical details about construction and maintenance.

**Haggling**: "The price is fair for the quality. You want cheap, there's plenty of other shops in the city."

**If treated with respect**: Becomes more helpful, might offer maintenance tips or recommendations for specific combat situations.

## Shop Details

**Location**: Trades Ward, Waterdeep
**Shop Name**: Ironforge Arms
**Specialty**: Quality melee weapons, weapon maintenance and repair
**Inventory**: Standard martial weapons, masterwork variants available for +50% cost

## Basic Statistics

AC: 14 (Chain shirt)

- Useful contact for weapon-related information
- Potential source for masterwork equipment
