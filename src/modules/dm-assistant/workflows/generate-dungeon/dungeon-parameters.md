# Dungeon Parameters Setup

## Basic Requirements

### Party Information
- **Party Level**: What level are the player characters?
- **Party Size**: How many players will participate?
- **Experience Level**: How familiar are players with D&D?
- **Preferred Challenges**: Combat, puzzles, roleplay, exploration?
- **Session Time**: How long do you want this dungeon to take?

### Campaign Integration
- **Campaign Setting**: What world/setting is this for?
- **Current Story Context**: How does this dungeon fit ongoing storylines?
- **Character Connections**: Do any PCs have relevant backstories?
- **Faction Involvement**: Are there relevant factions or organizations?
- **Consequences**: How will dungeon outcomes affect the broader campaign?

### Practical Constraints
- **Preparation Time**: How much time do you have to prepare?
- **Resources Available**: Books, maps, miniatures, digital tools?
- **Table Setup**: In-person, virtual, hybrid?
- **Follow-up Sessions**: Is this standalone or part of larger adventure?

## Dungeon Scope Definition

### Core Objectives
- **Primary Goal**: What must the party accomplish?
- **Secondary Goals**: What optional objectives exist?
- **Failure Stakes**: What happens if the party fails?
- **Success Rewards**: What do they gain for success?

### Challenge Balance
- **Combat Encounters**: How many fights do you want?
- **Puzzle Complexity**: Simple, moderate, or complex challenges?
- **Social Interaction**: NPCs to negotiate with or convince?
- **Exploration Elements**: Hidden areas, environmental challenges?

### Narrative Structure
- **Opening Hook**: How do characters learn about this dungeon?
- **Central Mystery**: What story unfolds as they explore?
- **Climactic Moment**: What's the dramatic high point?
- **Resolution**: How does the story conclude?

## Technical Specifications

### Encounter Guidelines
- **Easy Encounters**: 1-2 per dungeon for pacing
- **Medium Encounters**: 2-3 per dungeon as standard challenges  
- **Hard Encounters**: 1 per dungeon for climactic moments
- **Deadly Encounters**: Optional for experienced groups only

### Resource Management
- **Short Rest Opportunities**: Where can players recover?
- **Spell Component Needs**: Any special materials required?
- **Equipment Considerations**: Lighting, rope, tools needed?
- **Healing Availability**: Potions, NPCs, magical sources?

### Scaling Flexibility
- **Level Range**: What if players are higher/lower than expected?
- **Party Size Variation**: Adjustments for 3-6 players?
- **Difficulty Preference**: Options for easier/harder play?
- **Time Constraints**: Shorter/longer session adaptations?

## Quality Standards

### Player Engagement
- Each player should have meaningful spotlight time
- Multiple approaches should exist for major challenges
- Character abilities should be relevant and useful
- Player choices should meaningfully affect outcomes

### Narrative Satisfaction
- Story should feel complete regardless of specific outcomes
- Characters should feel heroic and capable
- Mysteries should have satisfying revelations
- Consequences should feel proportional to player actions

### Practical Usability
- You should be able to run this with minimal additional prep
- All necessary stats and information should be readily available
- Adjustments for unexpected situations should be straightforward
- Session should feel complete whether it runs short or long
