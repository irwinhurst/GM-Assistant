# Upload Adventure Content

## Supported Formats
- **PDF Pages**: Individual pages or sections from published adventures
- **Images**: Screenshots or scans of adventure content
- **Text Files**: Copied text from digital adventures

## What to Upload

### Essential Content
- **Area Descriptions**: Pages containing numbered areas with descriptions
- **Maps**: Visual layout of areas and their connections
- **Encounter Details**: Combat encounters within areas
- **NPC Information**: Important NPCs found in areas

### Upload Guidelines
- **High Resolution**: Ensure text is clearly readable
- **Complete Sections**: Upload full area descriptions, not partial text
- **Map References**: Include any maps that show area connections
- **Page Numbers**: Keep original page numbers visible for reference

## Processing Instructions

1. **Upload Files**: Drag and drop your adventure content
2. **Specify Range**: Indicate which areas/rooms you want to process
3. **Set Numbering**: Choose starting area number if different from source
4. **Map Key**: Specify if areas use letters, numbers, or mixed keys

## Example Content Types

### Room Descriptions
```
15. THRONE ROOM
A magnificent chamber with a raised dais at the far end...
Connections: North door leads to area 12, secret door to area 18...
```

### Area Maps
- Visual layouts showing connections between numbered areas
- Legend or key explaining symbols
- Scale and directional indicators

### Encounter Blocks
- Stat blocks for creatures in specific areas
- Trap descriptions with DCs and effects
- Environmental hazards

## Quality Tips
- **Clear Text**: Ensure all text is legible
- **Complete Context**: Include surrounding areas for connection context
- **Consistent Format**: Use consistent numbering/naming scheme
